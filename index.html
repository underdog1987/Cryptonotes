How it works

Every created note is a transaction in BSC network. Created notes are immutable, its content cannot be changed.

After the note is created, an unique ID is assigned to it. Be sure of save that ID, is the unique way to share and retrieve the note in the future!

Create and retrieve notes is absolutely free, just gas fees are applied.

Who can use it

Journalists, activists, politicians, and anyone who wants to share a public message

Sender and viewers only need a Metamask Wallet conected to Binance Smart Chain.

Main Features

  • Free. Store and retreive notes are free, just gas fee is applied.

  • Decentralized. Notes are saved in Binance Smart Chain, no risk of database lost.
    Everybody can create a client of Cryptonotes, just need clone repository and deploy it in web server. No serverside required!
